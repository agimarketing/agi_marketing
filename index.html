AGI Glossary

Welcome to the AGI Glossary. Below are important terms related to AGI and AI marketing:

AGI (Artificial General Intelligence)

AGI refers to machines that have general learning capabilities, comparable to human intelligence.

Neural Networks

Neural networks are systems of algorithms that mimic the human brain, commonly used in AI for pattern recognition.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data and improve over time without being explicitly programmed.
